Privacy Policy

At Pizza Hut, your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or place an order.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from you:

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Payment information for order processing.
  • Order history and preferences to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders efficiently.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries.
  • Send promotional offers, updates, and newsletters (if you opt-in).
  • Improve our website, services, and user experience.

Data Protection

We take the security of your information seriously. All sensitive data is encrypted and stored securely. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your preferences and improve our services. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, so please check back periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@pizzahut.com.
